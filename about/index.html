About

I am a software consultant focusing on Software Architecture and Functional programming. For over 20 years I've worked in many different types of systems with different technologies, it has been fun. During that time I created a collaboration tool called [Bytesize Architecture Sessions](https://www.roundcrisis.com/2021/09/28/bytesize-architecture-sessions/).

Along the way I played a key role in making [Kats Conf](http://web.archive.org/web/20170120102503/http://www.katsconf.com/), a programming conference, [Global GameCraft](https://www.gamecraft.it/) ,a community that runs game jams, and many other communities a reality. In the 2010s I co-founded BatCat Games, a PC and Console game development company in Ireland.
