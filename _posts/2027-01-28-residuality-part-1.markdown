##  Observations

The slow architectural review raised questions about preparation. It was unclear how much detail is necessary at the start and whether different preparation approaches would make the session smoother.

The decision about the naïve architecture highlighted how ambiguous this concept can feel when first learning the practice. It raised questions about how teams should define it and whether different interpretations affect the outcome.

The moment of defensiveness suggested that the exercise can surface emotional dynamics. It raised questions about how to create an environment where inquiry does not feel like evaluation.
