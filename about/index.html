About

Andrea is a professional software developer with 20 years of experience. Andrea played a key role in making Kats Conf, Global GameCraft and many other communities a reality. She co-founded BatCat Games, a PC and Console game development company in Ireland. These days she is a consultant / contractor focusing on strongly typed functional languages.

Andrea has been interested in the genesis of the tools she uses for quite some time, which derived into a keen interest into History and Philosophy of Computing. To that effect she researched and gave talks on the subject, such as:

  • A brief and incomplete history of programming languages - Curry On
  • Keynote: Inviting everyone to the party
